I am a postdoctoral researcher working on Algebraic Statistics at TU München in the Mathematical Statistics group led by Mathias Drton.

Previously I was a postdoc at the Max-Planck-Institut in Leipzig in the Nonlinear Algebra research group of Bernd Sturmfels and, before that, I spent some months at TU Berlin in the framework of the Thematic Einstein Semester on Algebraic Geometry.

I obtained my PhD in 2019 at Universitat de Barcelona on approximating local rings by Gorenstein rings under the supervision of Joan Elias.

My main research interests are in algebraic statistics, commutative algebra and their computational aspects.
