I obtained my PhD on July 2019 at Universitat de Barcelona under the supervision of Joan Elias. From August to December 2019, I stayed at TU Berlin for a short-term postdoc in the framework of the Thematic Einstein Semester on Algebraic Geometry under the supervision of Michael Joswig and Bernd Sturmfels. Since the start of 2020, I am a postdoc at the Max-Planck-Institut in Leipzig in the Nonlinear Algebra research group of Bernd Sturmfels.
